Elections are made accessible to all, including PWDs and senior citizens.

To be eligible to help, all participants must be of voting age. Members of the
EB who will be assisting these voters must also have completed sensitivity
training offered by the National Council on Disability Affairs and the
Commission on Human Rights.

There should be sign language interpreters or lists of candidates translated
into Braille in polling centers where voters with visual, speech, and hearing impairments are registered.

These measures are what RA 10366 and Comelec Resolution No. 10484 seek
to provide PWDs, seniors, and even pregnant women. But there is much room
for improvement. It is one of the solutions that Comelec established and as per
the Comelec resolution, APPs are supposed to cater to PWDs, senior citizens,
and heavily pregnant women who struggles to vote.
