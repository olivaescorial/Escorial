• The role of non-government organizations in governance is recognized.

• The party-list system of representation allows for marginalized and
underrepresented sectors to be represented in the national legislature.
The system serves as an attempt to depart from the personalistic character
of the political and electoral systems by giving focus to the party and its
issue-based platform.

• The Constitution and the 1991 Local Government Code (LGC) mandate
sectoral representation in the legislative bodies of local governments.

• People's participation is also provided for by the Local Government Code
through representation in consultative bodies such as the local health boards,
local school boards, local peace and order councils, and local development
councils at all local levels.
