{% extends 'boiler.html' %} {%block content%} {%load static%} Step 4: Cast Your Ballot


After filling out your ballot, keep it inside the ballot secrecy folder. Bring this
to the VCM and insert it into the machine.

Do not leave right away. Wait for the machine to process your ballot. Once
this is done, a poll clerk will mark your finger with indelible ink and will give
you your voting receipt to review.

Check your voting receipt to see if it correctly reflects who you voted for.

If the receipt does not match who you voted for, bring it to the BEI to register
your complaint. This will then be put on record.

If the receipt matches who you voted for, drop it in the voting receipt box once
done. Voters are not allowed to leave precincts with their voting receipt.
The Comelec says this is to protect the secrecy of the ballot and deter vote
buying.
